    About Charlie & Co.

    Welcome to Charlie & Co., your trusted partner in creating the perfect kitchen! Based in the heart of Ontario, Canada, we are proud to offer a wide selection of high-quality kitchen essentials, including premium kitchen cabinets, durable cookware, and versatile utensils. Whether you're outfitting your dream kitchen or updating your culinary tools, we’ve got you covered.

    At Charlie & Co., we believe the kitchen is more than just a place to cook—it’s the heart of the home where memories are made. That’s why we’re passionate about providing products that combine functionality, style, and value to suit every home and lifestyle. From sleek, modern cabinets to classic cookware and innovative utensils, our curated collections are designed to inspire creativity and enhance your cooking experience.

    We serve our customers both in-store and online, making it easy to explore our products and find what you need at your convenience. Our dedicated team is here to assist you every step of the way, ensuring you receive personalized service and expert advice tailored to your unique needs.

    At Charlie & Co., our mission is simple: to bring quality, comfort, and joy to your kitchen. Join us in making your kitchen the ultimate space for cooking, entertaining, and connecting with loved ones. Visit our store in Ontario or shop online today to discover why homeowners across Canada choose Charlie & Co. for their kitchen needs. Charlie & Co.—Your Kitchen, Perfected.
